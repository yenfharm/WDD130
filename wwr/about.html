Rafting Frayet Company

Raft down rivers with excitement and safety! Rafting Frayet Company is a worldwide based company offering exciting river trips for people of all ages and abilities..

History

"Rafting Frayet" was a rafting company founded in the 1980's by a family of rafting enthusiasts. The company was founded with the purpose of giving customers an unforgettable and safe rafting experience. This Rafting Company was dedicated to offering excursions throughout the country, with the highest priority placed on safety and fun.
