Helow

What do they want?: When customers visit a website about rafting, they're usually looking for specific information that can help them decide if they want to go rafting and, if so, which company to book with. They're usually looking for information about the location, the cost, the difficulty level of the rafting trip, and any other details that might be important to them.

What do they expect to find or what questions will be answered by them accessing the website?: Customers who visit a rafting website will usually have a few specific questions they want to have answered. They might be wondering:
  • Where is the rafting trip located? : Carabobo State
  • What is the difficulty level of the trip?: Easy and Medium
  • What kind of equipment is provided?: All the necessary for you healthy
  • How much does the trip cost? 250$

What are their needs that aren't being met?: Transportation back home
